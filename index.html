Hi there! I‚Äôm an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at Cornell. I lead the Praxis Lab, where we study how robots can learn to be robust and reliable in the real world.

My work integrates machine learning, numerical optimization, and control theory to study how robots can remain reliable when models, sensors, or hardware are imperfect.
