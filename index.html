pculbertson [at] stanford [dot] edu

I am a graduate student at Stanford, working in the Multi-Robot Systems Lab with Prof. Mac Schwager. I spend my summers as a visiting fellow at JPL. You can find my CV here.

I am interested in algorithms which enable teams of robots to learn about and modify their environment without centralization. This includes topics spanning (adaptive) control theory, learning and optimization for dynamical systems, and discrete optimization. I’m especially interested in fun applications like multi-robot manipulation and construction.
